Coffee+Doughnut is a Filipino style cafe that serves simple, natural food including Vegan and Gluten Free options accompanied by authentic espresso coffee. Most coffee shops in the United States don't do much with food and most restaurants don't worry about coffee. The Coffee+Doughnut is a hybrid combining a strong coffee culture and a mouth-watering doughnuts under one roof in a welcoming, cozy and comfortable neighborhood caf√©.

Our Coffee We serve a range of espresso coffees made from the finest coffee beans, roasted right here in Cape Coral, and our baristas really know their beans. With their superior technical knowledge and a passion for making amazing coffee they will make you the perfect cuppa.

Our Doughnuts are not your usual donuts around the corner. We use healthy and organic ingredients. We don't just use regular sugar, we use stevia for a low-calorie delights. We also have vegan and gluten-free doughnuts for your health conscious needs.
